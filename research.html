A central objective of the LPN-PPPL research is to understand the synergistic roles of plasma and nanoscale materials processes in plasma-based synthesis of nanomaterials. This research strongly emphasizes characterization of equilibrium and non-equilibrium plasmas as well as synthesized nanomaterials. A large collection of diagnostics is used to examine the plasma and material synthesis mechanisms, providing a database to validate the models and simulations before they are integrated into a multiscale model of plasma synthesis. These techniques require advances and improved understanding in plasma physics and materials science.
