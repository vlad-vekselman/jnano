Biography

Longtao Han received his Bachelor of Engineering degree from Materials Science and Engineering (MSE) department at Beihang University. Then he started to work on his PhD degree in MSE at Stony Brook University under supervision of Predrag Krstic (Stony Brook University) and Igor Kaganovich (PPPL), conducting theoretical research on plasma synthesis of CNT and BNNT via atomistic simulation and DFT calculation.
