Valerian Nemchinsky

Associated Professor
Keiser University, Fort Lauderdale, FL

Telephone: 954-927-1985

Email: vnemchinsky@keiseruniversity.edu

Research Interest

Plasma; Gas discharge; Electrode phenomena in arcs; Aerosol plasma; Heat and mass transfer during plasma processing; Plasma arc cutting; Welding; Plasma spraying.

Research and Professional Experience

  • 2007 - present: Associated Professor, Keiser University, Fort Lauderdale, FL
  • 2005 -2007: Associate Professor, BCC, Fort Lauderdale, FL
  • 1992-2005: Development associate, ESAB Welding and Cutting products, Florence, SC
  • 1989 -1991: Associate Professor, Bonch-Bruevich Technical University for Telecommunications, Leningrad, Russia
  • 1972-1989: Senior researcher, All Union Research Institute for Sources of Electricity (Leningrad Branch), Russia
  • 1966 -1972: Researcher, Institute for Semiconductors Russian Academy of Sciences, Leningrad, Russia
