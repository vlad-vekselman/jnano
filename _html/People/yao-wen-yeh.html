Biography

Originally from Taiwan, Yao-Wen Yeh received his BSc from the department of Physics, Rutgers University. Currently, he is pursuing his doctorate degree in the department of Electrical Engineering, Princeton University. Under the supervision of Dr. Yevgeny Raitses (PPPL), Prof. Bruce Koel (Princeton CBE), and Dr. Nan Yao (Princeton PRISM), Yao-Wen's research involves nanomaterial synthesis and characterizations.

Princeton Plasma Physics Laboratory is a U.S. Department of Energy national laboratory managed by Princeton University.
